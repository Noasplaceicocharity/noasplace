Noa’s Place Marketing & Communications Plan

1. Overview
Purpose:
 To raise awareness of Noa’s Place, build trust with families and partners, and position the charity as a hub for belonging and support.
Objectives:
Increase visibility among families, professionals, and local organisations.
Communicate values (kindness, belonging, listening, calm, growth).
Support fundraising and partnership development.
Drive engagement with events, resources, and the future hub.

2. Tone of Voice
Warm and Inclusive: Speak with empathy and clarity, avoiding jargon.
Empowering: Highlight strengths and possibilities with positive language.
Collaborative: Use “we” and “together” to reinforce community.
Calm and Reassuring: Reflect sensory-considerate spaces in language.
Neutral: Avoid judgment, criticism or bias 

3. Core Messaging
Who we are: A charity creating spaces of belonging for neurodiverse and disabled families.
What we do: Provide support, resources, and community through an inclusive hub.
Why it matters: No one should feel isolated; every family deserves to shine.
